A Hospital Bill Analyzer!



According to the Journal of Stroke and Cerebrovascular Diseases, 15% of all hospital patients are overcharged because of incorrect CPT codes. CPT Hospital Codes are a universal set of 5-digit codes that are associated with specific medical procedures. Unfortunately, incorrect CPT codes are billed, and people are charged much more than necessary. However, we have a solution to this impending problem. CodeToCash uses a CPT Code database that matches CPT codes with their correct prices, which exposes the inaccuracies of hospital bills. We hope to make CodeToCash a way to heal you without harming your wallet. Healthcare should be something that is affordable, and not a last resort.
